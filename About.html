ASTROLOGICAL SIGNS Astrology is a belief system and a study that seeks to understand and interpret the correlation between celestial phenomena and events or characteristics on Earth, including human experiences and personalities. It has been practiced in various forms by different cultures throughout history and is based on the idea that there is a connection between the positions and movements of celestial bodies (such as planets and stars) and the affairs of people and the natural world. It's important to note that astrology is not a science in the same sense as astronomy, which is the scientific study of celestial objects and phenomena. While astrology can be intriguing and enjoyable for some individuals, its interpretations and predictions should be taken with a grain of skepticism and viewed as a form of personal exploration rather than absolute truth.
